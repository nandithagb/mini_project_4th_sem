Harvest and post harvest

Depending on the duration of the crop, earhead grains maturity, yellowing of the straw, this can be harvested in two methods: If grown for seed production, harvest matured earheads, drying 3 – 4 days in the threshing yard and thresh by using motor operator machine/ tractor/ stone roller, followed by winnowing, cleaning and storing.
The crop grown under rainfed condition will be harvested along with earhead and straw, drying 3 – 4 days, making small bundles and stock in the field/ threshing yard for 20 – 25 days and thresh by using motor operator machine/ tractor/ stone roller, followed by winnowing, cleaning and storing.

Value addition

Ragi is important food crop, with processing and value addition can be prepared different highly nutritional foods which can help the community health management. Ragi has high proportion of protein, lime, iron and so on because of the processing products of ragi malt, savige, hurihittu, nippattu, Chakli, papad, pusti ragi laddu, millet soup sticks, bread, cute and sweet snacks that can be prepared in a bakery.
