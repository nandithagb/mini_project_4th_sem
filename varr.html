Special characters

* GPU- 26: Dwarf variety having high tillering capacity and blast tolerant.
*GPU- 45: Dwarf variety having higher tillering capacity, thin stem, and very good dry fodder for the livestock and suitable delayed sowing.
* GPU- 48: Suitable for delayed sowing in late kharif and possible to take up under irrigation where the farmers take 3 crops, tolerant to blast and good tillering capacity with brown coloured earheads.
*Indaf- 9: For late kharif, as well as for winter and summer.
*Indaf- 7: Can be grown throughout the year especially from October to January.
* Indaf- 5: Can be grown throughout the year except during winter months (October - January).
* HR- 911: Suited for both kharif and summer seasons, except for winter months for region III.
