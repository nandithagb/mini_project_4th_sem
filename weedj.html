Weed control

Hybrid jowar

*Irrigated: Spray 400 g. 50% WP Atrazin dissolved in 300 litres of water immediately after sowing or next day. There should be loosened soil with sufficient moisture in the soil.

Striga management

Deep ploughing, crop rotation with cotton and bunds and channel are free from striga.
At the time of striga flowering let the water into the plots for two days.
Spray 1 kg 80% W.P 2, 4 - D sodium salt dissolved in 300 litres of water on striga emerged from the stem portion of jowar crop.
