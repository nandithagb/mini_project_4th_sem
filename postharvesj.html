Harvest and post harvesting

Post notices The leaves turn yellow, drying and hardening of the grains.
Harvesting method Harvesting and drying of earheads.
Threshing Using improved farm machines/ tractor/ stone roller, threshing, cleaning, drying and storage.
Value addition Jowar comprising a nutritional products manufacturing value added and bakery products and in preparation of variety of foods recepies like roti, chapathi, laddu etc will be prepared. Sarghum is rich source of protein and important nutrients like iron, calcium, phosphorous etc that are necessary to keep the body healthy.
