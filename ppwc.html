WHEAT
h1>INSECTS
Insects pests Damage symptoms Control measures
Wheat stem borer *Infestation leads to formation of dead heart or chaffy earhead; such parts come out on pulling.
 *Spray Dimethoate 30 EC. @ 1.75 ml./liter water, when infestation noticed.
Aphids *Adults and nymphs suck the sap from leaves/tender ear heads. Infested leaves turn yellow colour, development of sooty mold on lower leaves which leads to stunted growth. If infestation on tender earheads shrivelled grains in the earhead. *If infestations in sever form spray Dimethoate 30 EC. at 1.75 ml./lit. water.
Leaf hoppers *White speckles on leaves followed by yellowing of leaves. *If infestations sever form spray Dimethoate 30 EC. at 1.75 ml./lit. water.

DISEASES

Diseases Damage symptoms Control measures
Smut disease *The earhead with blackish powdery mass of spores with out grain.
 *Seed treatment: Treat the seeds with Carboxin 75 WP.@ 2.0 g./kg.
OR *Soak the seeds in 50 degree C in hot water for 10 minutes.
Rust disease *Appearance of reddish coloured spots on leaves and stem. *Spray with 2.0 g.Mancozeb 75 WP./lit. water.
OR
Oxycarboxin 20 EC.@ 2.0 ml./lit. of water.
Leaf spot disease *Brown coloured spots; coalesce leads to drying of infested leaves. *Spray with 2.0 g. Mancozeb 75 WP./lit. water.
OR
Oxycarboxin 20 EC. @ 2.0ml./lit. of water.
