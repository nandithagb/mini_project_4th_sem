PUNAJI PADDY AREAS

This type of paddy is grown in zone - 5 (Kolar, Chikkaballapur, Ramanagar, Tumkur and Bangalore districts), and in a very limited areas in zone - 6 (Kollegala and Chamrajnagar taluks).
The crop is sown directly with the onset of rains in May and June, maintained as rain fed paddy until August, later on water is let - in and subsequently continued as wet rice.
It covers an estimated area of 0.3 lakh acre, with an average productivity of less than 6 quintal/ acre.
