WHEAT

Nutrient management

Apply 20 kg nitrogen, 30 kg phosphorous and 20 kg potash fertilizers to the soil at the time of sowing.
Topdress the crop with the remaining 20 kg nitrogen/acre at 30 days after sowing.

Water management/Intercultivation

Irrigate the crop once in 8 - 10 days in case of sandy loam soils and once in 15 days in case of black soils.
The critical periods are crown root initiation (20 - 25 days after sowing), tillering, flowering and grain formation.
Keep the field free from weeds 15 days after sowing followed by 2 - 3 intercultivations at 10 days interval.

Weed management

Spray 2, 4 - D sodium salt 80% WP.at 1 kg / acre dissolved in 300 litres of water, when the crop is in 5 - 7 leaves stage.
or Spray Methazole 75% WP. at 400 g./acre dissolved in 300 litres of water, 25 days after sowing.
