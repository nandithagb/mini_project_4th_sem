SAMRUDHI

contains Nutri Cereal Ragi which has been a part of Traditional foods in major parts of India, being grown for over 4000 years in India. Ragi is also locally known by different names like nachni, mandua, keppai, taidalu, finger millet etc. With over 14 Vitamins & Minerals and key Immuno nutrients like Vitamin A, C, D, Iron and Zinc, this cereal helps support normal immune system function.

AVANI

Pre-emergence application of bensulfuron-methyl + pretilachlor, butachlor, isoproturon and post-emergence application of 2,4-D, chlorimuron- ethyl either alone or in combination with other methods were found effective in managing weeds in finger millet
PRANA

Finger Millet, also known as Ragi is an important millet grown extensively in various regions of India and Africa. Its scientific name is Eleusine coracana. It ranks sixth in production after wheat, rice, maize, sorghum and bajra in India.
THANJAI

Finger millet is an excellent source of calcium whose consumption from childhood builds healthy bones. Ragi has high protein content & rich source of minerals. Ragi mixed with salt and water can be steamed as puttu and eaten as a savoury or sweet breakfast dish.
