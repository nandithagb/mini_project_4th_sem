SONA

Sona Moti Wheat. is a 2000-year-old desi variety of emmer wheat, grown without the use of fertilizers or pesticides. It is low in gluten and high in folic acid, rich in protein, magnesium and iron. It helps lower blood sugar levels while helping build immunity.
MALDANDI

Soft Bhakri as compared to Desi Jowar Bhakri. ₹53.00 – ₹93.00. Cultivated extensively throughout India and because of its Softer bhakri/roti has become a local favourite too. These jowar seeds are larger in size as compared to Desi/Dagdi Jowar.
SORGHAM

Sorghum grain is a feed crop in industrial nations and a food crop in several developing countries. Much of the sorghum grain used as food is used in porridge; relatively little is milled into flour. Its milling is similar to that of barley. Whole grain sorghum flour is used in the making of a bread, roti, in India.
C S V

Wheat seed is an oblong shape with a blunt tip. The seed is gold to pale yellow in colour, with a papery texture. It is 6mm in length. The wheat seedling produces a slender coleoptile, followed by a true leaf

MADU

Madu mulungi rice (also known as Rosematta rice, Palakkadan Matta rice, Kerala Red rice, or Red parboiled rice) is an indigenous variety of rice grown in Palakkad District of Kerala. It is different from brown rice.
