Ragi leaf and neck blast Disease may occur from the seedling stage to the heading stage. On leaves spindle shaped greyish green spots with whitish margins of different sizes; may coalesce and such leaves start drying. When infestation occurs on neck or fingers; such earheads becomes chaffy (chaffy earhead) and infested fingers becomes chaffy. When infestation noticed spray the crop with 2.0 g. Mancozeb 75 WP.
OR
1.0 g. Carbendazim 50 WP.
OR
1.0 ml. Edifenphos 50 EC.
OR
2.5 g. Zineb 75 WP./lit. of water on15 days old nursery. Repeat the spray 20 - 30 days after transplanting and when 50 per cent ear head emergence.
Brown leaf spot Appearance of egg shaped spots. Spray 1.0 g. Carbendazim 50 WP.
OR
1.0 Mancozeb 75 WP. in a lit. water when infestation noticed. 300 liters. of spray mixture per acre.
Mottle streak viral disease Appearance of green and yellow strips on leaves. Pull out infested plants and burn.
To control the vector. Spray 1.7 ml.
Dimethoate 30 EC./lit. of water.
Seed dressing: Treat the seeds with 2.0 g.
of Carbendazim 50 WP. /kg. of seeds before sowing.
