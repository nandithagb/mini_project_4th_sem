Nutrient management

Hybrid jowar

-* irrigated: Before sowing apply 20 kg nitrogen, 30 kg phosphorous and 16 kg potash fertilizers to the soil and remaining 20 kg nitrogen 4 weeks after sowing as topdressing.
*Earthup the crop after topdressing.

Hybrid jowar

-* rainfed: Before sowing apply of 13 kg nitrogen, 16 kg phosphorous and 16 kg potash fertilizers to the soil and remaining 13 kg nitrogen 4 weeks after sowing as topdressing.

Ear head midge *Fail to grain formation in the earhead, only empty glumes present. *When 10 per cent earhead emerged spray 2.0 ml. Quinalphos 25 EC. /lit. water.
OR
* 2.0 ml. Chlorpyriphos 20 EC./lit. water.
*Repeat the spray after 50 per cent earheads emergence. 250 lit. spray solution/acre.
Root grub *Grubs feeds on rootlets, drying of such plants, easily come out of soil by pulling. *Collect the beetles after April - May rain during dark hours.
*Repeated deep ploughing during June - July.
*At the time of sowing incorporate 5 kg. Phorate 10 G./acre in plough furrow.
* If water available mix 0.4 ml. Chlorpyriphos 20 EC. per m2 in irrigation water.
Red spider mite *Large numbers of mites construct the delicate webs and lacerate the green mater from the leaves. Such leaves with reddish spots. *Spray 2.5 ml. Dicofol- 18.5 EC./lit. water, spray solution should reach ventral surface of the leaf. 250 lit. spray solution/acre
