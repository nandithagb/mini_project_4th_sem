RICE

Harvesting and Post Harvesting

Harvest the crop at 90% of grain maturity.
Separation of grains immediately after harvesting.
Restrict the drying of paddy grains about 4 - 6 hours at each time,other else rice will be broken.

Value addition

Rice is the most widely consumed staple food for a large part of the world's human population.
Value-added processing of rice bran to increase the recovery of anthocyanins or oil potentially impacts human health, energy concerns and environmental issues.>
