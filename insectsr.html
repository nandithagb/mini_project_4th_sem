Insect pests Damage symptoms Control measures
Stem borer Caterpillar bore the base of the stem and feed the internal content, such infested plants shown the symptoms of dead heart during vegetative stage and chaffy earheads if infestation occurs during heading stage.
 Spray Chlorpyriphos 20 EC. two ml./lit. water at 25 - 30 days after transplanting. If infestation noticed repeat the spray. 250 lit. of spray solution required per acre.
Aphids Large number of individuals sucks the sap from leaves and tender earheads, yellowing of leaves; development of sooty mold; and shrivelled grain.
 Spray Dimethoate 30 EC. @ 1.7 ml./lit. of water when infestation occurs. 250 litres of spray solution required/acre.
Earhead caterpillar Caterpillar cut the tender grains, broken grains and excretory pellets accumulated with in the earhead or may be on ground.
 Dust Malathion 5 D. on earheads during morning hours at 8 - 10 kg./acre.
Root grub Grubs eat the roots such infested hills dries up.
 In the month of April - May incorporate 8 kg. Carbofuran 3 G.
